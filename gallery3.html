• Mandalas Gallery


I enjoy rendering what I imagine and observe. I love to play around with new ideas and explore deeper. I have created many mandalas (sacred geometry patterns) with calligraphy (light codes). I see the idea in my mind's eye and then create the geometry and add the details. When I channel my artwork I feel calm, peaceful, and connected with everything. Without any thoughts I abide in the love of my heart and meditate.

For some of the mandalas I have used a WACOM graphics tablet with Adobe Photoshop to add calligraphic detail to some of the mandalas. I have also used different software apps to enhance the graphics. The apps I have used Kalieder, Photospiralysis, and Omnigeometry to create visionary art.
