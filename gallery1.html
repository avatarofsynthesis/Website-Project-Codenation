• Artwork Gallery


My creative passion and experimentation has led me to bring in multi-dimensional light and colour into my artwork. When I channel light through optical media I utilise a 'liquid medium' where no two images are the same. Each picture has been taken from a different angle, has a different texture, and a unique pattern of light. These variations are due to the unique angle of light, camera and material. The light hitting the camera's lens can play tricks on the camera and the eyes, and optical effects also make great pictures.

My artwork gallery is a selection of drawings, paintings, and artistic photography. The art I have created with pastels, pencil, and paint was scanned in and enhanced with Adobe Photoshop with gradients and other effects added. It is the thrill of capturing something new and unique that inspires me. It is so refreshing to go out into nature, to snap a few hundred pics and then play with them enhancing their vibrancy and energy.
